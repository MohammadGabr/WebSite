Products

Cure is cloud based health care solution that covers all the participants in the health system: patients , doctors , clinics , hospitals , pharmacies and labs. We aim to set a common platform between them all in order to provide best communication channels between them with minimizing time and cost and providing rich detailed information.
Cure consists of (current phase) :

  • Clinic managment system : manage the daily operations of clinic
  • Patient App: view visits , book visit , view Receipts
  • Doctor App : view and edit visits , view clinic calendar, send messages to patients
  • Secretary App : Book visits to doctor
  • Pharmacy Receipts system : Scan Receipts
  • Lab Receipts system : Scan eReceipts
* All Apps support all mobile platforms
